The Importance of Freshly Made Naan in Indian Cuisine

Naan is an iconic part of Indian cuisine, with its soft, pillowy texture and deliciously smoky flavor. Traditionally, naan is cooked in a tandoor, a clay oven that imparts a unique taste to the bread, making it a beloved choice for many food lovers. Naan pairs wonderfully with various Indian dishes such as curries, kebabs, and tandoori specialties.

At Top of India, the restaurant takes great pride in crafting their naan from scratch daily, ensuring each piece is fresh and flavorful. Whether you choose classic naan, garlic naan, or cheese naan, you can count on a warm, fluffy bread that perfectly complements your meal.

But the balance of flavors and textures truly makes freshly made naan in Spokane at the Top of India exceptional. The naan is perfectly chewy inside with a crisp, golden exterior, making it the ideal companion for the restaurant’s flavorful and aromatic curries.

Experience Traditional Indian Flavors at Top of India

While the naan is undoubtedly a highlight, the rest of the menu at Top of India is equally impressive. The restaurant offers a wide range of dishes that showcase the richness and diversity of Indian cuisine. From savory appetizers like samosas and pakoras to tender curries and flavorful biryanis, each dish is prepared with attention to detail and a focus on authentic flavors.

Some popular dishes to try alongside your naan include:

Butter Chicken

A favorite among many, butter chicken is a rich, creamy curry made with tender chicken pieces and a luscious tomato-based sauce. It’s the perfect dish to enjoy with a warm piece of naan to scoop up all the delicious sauce.

Lamb Rogan Josh

For those who love lamb, Rogan Josh is a must-try. This dish features tender lamb cooked in a fragrant curry sauce with a blend of aromatic spices. Pair it with your naan for an unforgettable meal.

Tandoori Chicken

Cooked in the traditional tandoor, tandoori chicken is a flavorful and smoky dish that pairs perfectly with naan. The chicken is marinated in a yogurt-based mixture and seasoned with a variety of spices before being roasted to perfection.

Paneer Tikka

For vegetarian guests, paneer tikka is a great option. This dish features cubes of paneer (Indian cheese) marinated in a blend of spices and grilled until golden and slightly crispy. The flavor-packed paneer pairs wonderfully with naan and a refreshing side of raita.

Biryani

Top of India also serves up aromatic and flavorful biryanis. Whether you prefer chicken, lamb, or vegetarian biryani, each dish is prepared with basmati rice, saffron, and a variety of spices for a satisfying and aromatic meal.
